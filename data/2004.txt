Youth Suicides Commited Hourly
http://blogos.com/article/27174/
The suicide data uploaded on the National Police Agency's site contains data from 2004 onwards. Between 2004 and 2007 the details of these suicides have yet to been found. However there is a large entry suspecting that it may be related to loss of jobs, overwork, and other work-related problems that have caused these suicides.
